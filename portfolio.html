Music Player

This is a simple music app I designed using UWP (Universal Windows Platform) and Firebase for storage. The app allows you to store your favorite songs, create playlists, and enjoy music directly from the app. Additionally, I added podcast functionality so users can listen to refreshing podcasts right from the app. Here are some key features:

  • Music Storage: Upload and save your favorite songs to your personal library using Firebase for storage.
  • Playlists: Create and manage playlists with your favorite tracks for easy access and playback.
  • Podcasts: Stream podcasts directly from the app for entertainment or educational content.
  • Offline Mode: Listen to your music and podcasts even when you don't have an internet connection.

A Simple Hotel Management Software

I developed a Simple Hotel Management System using MVC, MVVM, and Bootstrap for the design. This system enables customers to book functions and use equipment, providing a seamless experience for event planning and management. The application also supports the upload and download of function details and equipment prices through Excel and CSV formats, streamlining data management for hotel administrators.

  • Function Booking: Allows customers to book functions at the hotel.
  • Equipment Use: Customers can reserve and use hotel equipment for their events.
  • Excel/CSV Upload: Admins can upload event and equipment data through Excel or CSV files.
  • Download Function Details: Allows downloading details of booked functions and equipment pricing for customer reference.

Contoso Retailers

The Contoso Retailer Report provides a comprehensive analysis of retail performance, enabling users to identify best-selling and lagging products, analyze profitability by sales channels, and determine the most profitable time periods. This report is designed to support strategic decision-making for improving sales and operational efficiency.

  • Best and Worst-Selling Products: Displays product performance to identify top-performing and underperforming items.
  • Profitability Analysis: Breaks down sales data by channels and products to highlight areas of high and low profitability.
  • Time Period Insights: Analyzes sales trends over time to identify the most profitable periods.
  • Dynamic Visualizations: Interactive charts and tables provide actionable insights for decision-making.
